# 01: Sarah's Weekend Rescue

**Project:** festgrid
**Created:** 2026-07-20
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Find a suitable family-friendly event and add it to her calendar, feeling confident and relieved.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** Increase user engagement.
**Objective:** Users add avg. 2 events/month.

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Sarah, The Busy Parent (Primary)
**Situation:** It's Friday evening, she's tired from a long week, and she realizes she hasn't planned anything for the weekend with her kids.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** To quickly find a fun, affordable, nearby event that her kids will love.

**Worry:** Wasting hours searching, finding nothing suitable, and ending up with bored, unhappy kids.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Mobile
**Entry:** Opens the FestGrid app/website directly.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
In under 5 minutes, she finds a local street fair for Saturday afternoon, sees it's free, and adds it to her family's shared calendar with one click.

**Business Success:**
A core user is retained through a successful, low-friction interaction. The "events added to calendar" KPI is positively impacted.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1.  **Home/Event Discovery** — Sees "This Weekend's Top Family Events".
2.  **Event Detail** — Clicks on an event and scans the key details.
3.  **(Action) Add to Calendar** — Clicks "Add to Calendar" and confirms. ✓
