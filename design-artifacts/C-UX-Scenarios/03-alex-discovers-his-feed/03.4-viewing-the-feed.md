# 03.4-viewing-the-feed

## Page Metadata

| Property | Value |
|---|---|

| **Platform** | Web (Desktop) |

## Overview

**Page Purpose:** To display a personalized feed of upcoming events aggregated from the user's subscriptions.

**Entry Context:** Alex has just completed the subscription wizard and has been redirected back to his main feed (scenario 03.3).

**Exit Action:** Alex finds an interesting event and clicks on it to view more details, transitioning to the Event Detail page.

## User Situation

Alex has successfully subscribed to "The Weekend Warriors" and is now looking at his feed, expecting to see their upcoming shows.

## Mental State

-   **Eager and Expectant:** He is excited to see if his new subscription has yielded any interesting events.
-   **Goal-Oriented:** He is actively looking for events to attend.

## On-Page Interactions

This page implements the standard **Event List View** pattern, as defined in `design-artifacts/D-Design-System/01-event-list-view.md`.

### Context-Specific Behaviors:

*   **Default Filter:** The feed is automatically filtered to show events **only from the user's subscribed accounts**.
*   **Feed-Specific Filters:** In addition to the standard filters, there may be an option to filter by specific subscriptions if the user has multiple.
