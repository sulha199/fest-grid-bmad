## Overview

**Page Purpose:** To see a list of relevant events, defaulting to what's nearby, and allowing for powerful search and filtering to narrow down the options.

**Entry Context:** Opens the FestGrid app/website directly.

**Exit Action:** Clicks on an event.

**On-Page Interactions:**
*   **Defaults to "Nearby" (with fallback):** Upon opening, the app attempts to show events near Sarah's primary saved location. If she has no locations saved, or if no events are found nearby, it defaults to showing all upcoming events to avoid an empty screen.
*   **Free-Text Search:** Taps the search bar and types "puppet show" to find specific events.
*   **Filter by Location:** Taps the location filter, which shows her list of saved locations ("Home", "Kids' School"). She selects "Kids' School" to see events near there.
*   **Filter by Category**: Taps on "Family & Kids" category from the Filter Hub.
*   **Filter by Type**: Taps on "Workshop" type from the Filter Hub.
*   The event grid updates to show events matching all selected filters.
*   **Quick Favorite:** Taps the heart icon on an event card directly in the list to quickly save it for later, without leaving the discovery page.
*   Scrolls through the filtered event list.

## User Situation
It's Friday evening, she's tired from a long week, and she realizes she hasn't planned anything for the weekend with her kids.

## Mental State
-   **Hope:** To quickly find a fun, affordable, nearby event that her kids will love.
-   **Worry:** Wasting hours searching, finding nothing suitable, and ending up with bored, unhappy kids.

Opens the FestGrid app/website directly.
